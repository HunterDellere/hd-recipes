steps:
  - text: 'Stub: rinse the 400 g raw glutinous rice in a bowl of cold water, agitating with the fingers, until the water runs from cloudy to mostly clear, 3 to 4 changes. Cover with 1500 ml / 6 cups cold water and soak 4 to 8 hours (or overnight). The soak hydrates the dense glutinous-rice starch through the grain; an unsoaked grain steams unevenly with a hard chalky center.'
    time_min: 240
  - text: 'Stub: drain the soaked rice, rinse once more, and shake dry. Line a bamboo steamer basket (a Thai/Lao huat) with cheesecloth or pre-dampened muslin, mound the rice in the center in a single dome about 5 cm / 2 inches thick. Set the basket over a pot or a thai sticky-rice steamer pot of simmering water (the rice should sit above, not touching, the water surface).'
    time_min: 5
